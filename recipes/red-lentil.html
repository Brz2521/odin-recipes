Red Lentil Pasta

Red lentil pasta is a vegetarian option for a healthy meal. It is an excellent option, if you are looking to increase your protein intake. It is rich in fiber, gluten-free and has a low glycemic index.

Here you will be able to make this dish yourself by following the ingredients and steps:

  • Red lentil pasta (any form of red lentil pasta of your choice)

    • For the sauce

  • 60g unsalted, unroasted cashews
  • 1 large garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 roasted from a jar, large red pepper drained, no seeds
  • 4 sundried tomatoes
  • 8 large basil leaves, chopped

Instructions

1. Prepare the cashews

  • Place the cashews in a bowl and cover with hot water. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes. This will soften them and make them easier to blend.

2. Cook the Pasta

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the red lentil fusilli and cook according to the package instructions, typically around 7-9 minutes, until al dente.
  • Drain the pasta and set aside.

3. Make the Sauce

  • While the pasta is cooking, drain the soaked cashews and add them to a blender or food processor.
  • Add the roughly chopped garlic clove, roasted red pepper, sundried tomatoes, and basil leaves to the blender.
  • Blend until smooth, adding a small amount of water if needed to achieve a creamy consistency. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

4. Combine Pasta and Sauce

  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta with the cashew and roasted red pepper sauce. Toss well to ensure the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.

5. Serve and Enjoy

  • Serve immediately and enjoy your healthy and delicious red lentil pasta dish!
