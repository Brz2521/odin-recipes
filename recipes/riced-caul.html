Riced Cauliflower Sausage Bowl Recipe

  • 1 cup iced cauliflower
  • 2 oz chorizo sausage (or a mix of pork and beef sausage)
  • 1/2 cup cooked spinach
  • 1/2 poblano pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced mango
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped white onion
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 dried guajillo pepper, crushed
  • 1/2 jalapeno pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

1. Saut√© the Vegetables and Meat:

  • Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped white onion, poblano pepper, and jalapeno pepper.
  • Cook until the onions become translucent and the peppers start to soften, about 5 minutes.
  • Add the chorizo sausage to the skillet. Cook, breaking it up into smaller pieces, until it is fully cooked and browned.

2. Add Spices and Riced Cauliflower:

  • Once the meat is cooked, add the paprika, crushed dried guajillo pepper, and garlic powder. Stir to combine.
  • Add the riced cauliflower to the skillet. Mix well to incorporate all the spices and ingredients.

3. Cook Pumpkin Seeds:

  • Add the pumpkin seeds to the skillet. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the cauliflower is tender and the pumpkin seeds are lightly toasted, about 5-7 minutes.

4. Incorporate Tomatoes and Tomato Paste:

  • Add the diced tomatoes and tomato paste to the skillet. Stir to combine and cook for another 3-5 minutes until the tomatoes start to soften and release their juices.

5. Finish with Spinach and Mango:

  • Add the cooked spinach and apple cider vinegar to the skillet. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  • Finally, add the diced mango. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, just enough to heat the mango without letting it dissolve.

6. Serve:

  • Remove the skillet from heat and squeeze fresh lime juice over the dish.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful riced cauliflower sausage bowl!
