Riced Cauliflower Sausage Bowl Recipe

  • 1 cup iced cauliflower
  • 2 oz chorizo sausage (or a mix of pork and beef sausage)
  • 1/2 cup cooked spinach
  • 1/2 poblano pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced mango
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped white onion
  • 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 dried guajillo pepper, crushed
  • 1/2 jalapeno pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
