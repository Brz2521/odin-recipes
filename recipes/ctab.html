Compari Tomato Avocado Toast with Bacon

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • 1/3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 Compari tomatoes
  • 2 tbs Thyme
  • 2 Sourdough bread slices
  • 3 Bacon strips

Instructions

1. Avocado spread

  • Mash a soft avocado into a bowl with a fork

2. Roast the Compari Tomatoes

  • Slice the compari tomatoes on a cutting board.
  • Take aluminum foil and cover it on an oven pan.
  • Place the compari tomatoes on the pan with the aluminum foil.
  • Add 1/3 tbsp of olive oil on the tomatoes.
  • Add 2 tps of thyme.
  • Put the sliced tomatoes in the oven for 10 min until they are roasted.

3. Cook the Bacon

  • Chop 3 strips of bacon (pork or turkey) and cook it in a pan on a stove.

4. Bread and Avocado spread

  • Toast the 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • After you've mashed the avocado it's time to spread it onto the two slices of sourdough bread
  • Add the compari tomatoes and the chopped cooked bacon.

5. Enjoy

  • Place the toasts onto a plate and enjoy!
