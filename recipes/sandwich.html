PB and J Sandwich

To make the Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich, you will need these ingredients:

  • Two slices of bread
  • Peanut butter spread
  • Jelly (any jam you like)

Now to make the sandwich

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Put the two slices of bread of the spread together
